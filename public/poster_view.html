Conflict Nearing Turkish Border.
by Delilah Jones
5:30pm Oct 5th, 2014
West Bank, Israrl

For the fifth day in a row, conflict between Syrian Kurds and ISIL fighters have been audible and visible from the Turkish/Syrian border. Authorities in Turkish border towns have also reported streams of incoming Syrian Kurds moving across the border, seeking refuge from the intense fighting that seems to be taking a serious toll on the Kurdish forces.


Speculation in local media has hinted that the Turkish national government is facing pressure from the US to broaden its role, given recent events.
